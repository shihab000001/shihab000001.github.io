01 — About

About Me

I'm an Electrical & Electronic Engineering undergraduate working across power systems, control theory, digital electronics, and microwave engineering — while pursuing a research-level interest in superconducting quantum circuits on the side. I like building understanding from first principles: before I trust a simulation, I want the derivation on paper.

My long-term goal is to pursue graduate research in superconducting quantum computing and quantum hardware — bridging circuit design, microwave engineering, and quantum information theory.
